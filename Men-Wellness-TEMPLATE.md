## PAGE STRUCTURE (Section by Section)

---

### SECTION 1 — Sticky Header Bar
**Descriptive Name:** Urgency / Contact Sticky Bar

**DR Purpose:**  
Anchors a phone CTA at the very top of every scroll position. Removes friction — the prospect never has to hunt for how to buy. Creates urgency by labelling it "Free Consultation" (lowers commitment perception).

**Copy Formula:**  
- Micro-commitment softener: "Free Consultation"  
- Direct phone CTA with number visible

**Design Pattern:**  
- Full-width dark bar (near-black `#1a1a1a` or deep charcoal)  
- Centered text + phone icon + phone number  
- Sticky (fixed position, always visible)  
- Font: Bold, white/cream  
- No other links — single focus

**Template Copy:**
```
Free Consultation
📞 Call Now : [PHONE_NUMBER]
```

---

### SECTION 2 — Scrolling Ticker / Announcement Bar
**Descriptive Name:** Social Proof Ticker

**DR Purpose:**  
Creates FOMO and momentum. Continuous movement draws the eye and plants the key value proposition subliminally even before the hero loads fully. Borrowed credibility through repetition.

**Copy Formula:**  
- Desire trigger (outcome) + Identity claim, repeated in loop  
- No specific offer — pure desire amplification

**Design Pattern:**  
- Full-width amber/gold background (`#F0A500` or similar warm gold)  
- White bold text, marquee/ticker animation scrolling left  
- Alternates between two short phrases  
- High contrast — breaks the dark background above

**Template Copy:**
```
[HERO_CLAIM] • [AUDIENCE] की पहली पसंद • [HERO_CLAIM] • [AUDIENCE] की पहली पसंद •
```
*(Loops continuously)*

---

### SECTION 3 — Hero Section
**Descriptive Name:** Hero: Desire + Proof Overlay

**DR Purpose:**  
The make-or-break first impression. Must answer "what is this for me?" within 3 seconds. Uses aspirational imagery (bedroom = implied intimacy/performance context) paired with a floating card that delivers the headline, social proof number, and a CTA — all above the fold.

**Copy Formula:**  
- **Pattern Interrupt Headline:** Bold outcome statement, no product name yet. Speaks to the desire, not the product.  
- **Amplification Sub-headline:** Triples down on the desire with more specifics (More X, More Y, More Z structure)  
- **Soft Authority Note:** Ingredient/origin badge (adds intrigue, differentiates)  
- **Social Proof Micro-block:** Star rating + user count (mass validation)  
- **Primary CTA:** Single action, high-contrast button

**Design Pattern:**  
- Full-viewport hero image (dark/moody lifestyle photo — bedroom, candles, warm tones)  
- Right-aligned frosted/semi-transparent dark card overlay (`rgba(30,30,30,0.85)`)  
- Headline: Extra-large bold white text (serif or heavy sans)  
- Sub-headline: Medium weight, white  
- Rating: Star icons (amber/gold) + count text  
- CTA Button: Amber/gold (`#F0A500`), rounded pill, black bold text, full-width within card  
- Second CTA button floats at bottom of viewport (redundant CTA pattern)

**Template Copy:**
```
HEADLINE:     [HERO_CLAIM]
SUB-HEADLINE: [SUB_CLAIM]

Badge:        ([KEY_INGREDIENT])

Rating:       ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ [OVERALL_RATING] Rating By [TOTAL_REVIEWS] Users

CTA:          [CALL NOW / ORDER NOW / GET YOURS]
```

### SECTION 4 — Product Introduction / Credibility Bridge
**Descriptive Name:** What Is It? — Origin Story & Global Authority

**DR Purpose:**  
Handles the "never heard of it" objection before it becomes a barrier. Bridges the gap from emotional desire (Section 3) to rational understanding. Establishes global credibility and local relevance simultaneously. Plants the "trusted worldwide, now in India" authority hook.

**Copy Formula:**  
- **Category Definition:** "What is [PRODUCT]?" — direct, no fluff  
- **Origin Story + Global Social Proof:** "[Product] is trusted by [millions worldwide / specific number]"  
- **Local Relevance Bridge:** "Now available in [country/region]"  
- **Dual Language:** Primary language for emotional resonance + English for authority/aspiration

**Design Pattern:**  
- Clean section, lighter background (dark grey or white, contrasting from hero)  
- Two text blocks: Hindi (larger, emotional) + English (smaller, rational)  
- No images in this section — text focus to let copy land  
- CTA button (⚡) at bottom of this section

**Template Copy:**
```

### SECTION 5 — Expert Video Carousel
**Descriptive Name:** Expert Validation Carousel

**DR Purpose:**  
Third-party credibility at its most powerful. Video format implies authenticity (hard to fake). Multiple experts (3+) multiplies perceived authority. Carousel format encourages active engagement and dwell time on page.

**Copy Formula:**  
- **Authority Transfer:** "Expert Views" headline signals this is not the brand talking  
- Carousel implies multiple independent sources of endorsement  
- No scripted pitch — the visual format does the persuasion

**Design Pattern:**  
- Section heading: "Expert Views" (or "What Experts Say")  
- Video thumbnail carousel, 3 slides, auto-plays or click-to-play  
- Dark/neutral background to keep focus on video  
- Navigation arrows (prev/next)  
- Minimal text — visuals do the work

**Template Copy:**
```

### SECTION 6 — Why Choose Us (Differentiators)
**Descriptive Name:** Objection Handler — Why This Over Others

**DR Purpose:**  
Pre-empts comparison shopping. Addresses the top 4 rational objections a sceptical buyer has before committing: "Is it safe? Is it natural? Does it really work on all my issues? Can I trust this brand?" Each block kills one objection with a benefit statement.

**Copy Formula:**  
- **Category Question Framing:** "Why Choose [PRODUCT] Over Other [CATEGORY] Supplements?"  
- **Empathy opener:** Acknowledges the difficulty of choosing  
- **4-Point Differentiator Grid:**  
  1. Global Trust (social proof)  
  2. Natural/Safe Ingredients (risk removal)  
  3. Multi-action Formula (breadth of benefit)  
  4. No Side Effects (safety guarantee)

**Design Pattern:**  
- 4 blocks in a 2x2 grid or vertical list  
- Each block: Bold label (title) + 1-2 sentence explanation  
- Icons or checkmarks optional but recommended  
- CTA button (⚡) at end of section

**Template Copy:**
```
HEADING: Why Choose [PRODUCT_NAME] Over Other [AUDIENCE] Health Supplements?

INTRO: Choosing the right [AUDIENCE] health supplement can be overwhelming, but here's why [PRODUCT_NAME] stands out:

BLOCK 1 — Global Trust
Title: [TRUST_LABEL] (e.g. "दुनिया भर में भरोसेमंद")
Body:  [PRODUCT_NAME] को दुनियाभर में इस्तेमाल किया जा रहा है, और यह एक ग्लोबल फेवरेट बन चुका है।

BLOCK 2 — Natural Purity  
Title: शुद्ध [INGREDIENT_TYPE] और 100% प्राकृतिक जड़ी-बूटियाँ
Body:  यह शुद्ध [KEY_INGREDIENT] से बना है, जिसमें कोई हानिकारक केमिकल या मिलावट नहीं है।

BLOCK 3 — Multi-Action Formula
Title: [OUTCOME_CATEGORY] के लिए मल्टी-एक्शन फॉर्मूला
Body:  यह आपकी [BENEFIT_1], [BENEFIT_2], [BENEFIT_3], [BENEFIT_4] और [BENEFIT_5] को नैचुरली बढ़ाने में मदद करता है।

BLOCK 4 — Safety/No Side Effects

[CTA BUTTON: CALL NOW → CTA_LINK]
```

### SECTION 7 — Benefits / Advantages List
**Descriptive Name:** Benefit Stack — The Outcome Promise

**DR Purpose:**  
Converts desire into specific expected outcomes. Each bullet is a mini-promise tied to a specific ingredient or mechanism (adds scientific believability). The numbered list format makes benefits feel comprehensive, not cherry-picked. More benefits = more people see themselves in the solution.

**Copy Formula:**  
- **Section Heading:** "Advantages" / "Benefits"  
- **Intro Line:** Frames benefits as comprehensive wellness improvement  
- **Benefit Bullets (7+):** Format = **Bold Benefit Name:** Mechanism + Outcome  
  - Always ties back to ingredient when possible (credibility anchor)  
  - Covers multiple desire dimensions: physical, emotional, relational

**Design Pattern:**  
- Bulleted list (custom icons or large bullets in amber/gold)  
- Bold label + regular weight explanation on same line  
- Generous line spacing  
- CTA button (⚡) at section end

**Template Copy:**
```
HEADING: Advantages / [PRODUCT_NAME] के फायदे

INTRO: Our [AUDIENCE] health supplements offer a range of benefits designed to improve your overall [AUDIENCE] wellness:

BULLET FORMAT:

EXAMPLE BULLETS (replace with your benefits):
• [HORMONE] को नैचुरली बढ़ाता है: शरीर में [HORMONE] की मात्रा को बढ़ाकर ऊर्जा और जोश को बढ़ाता है।
• बेहतर [PHYSICAL_OUTCOME]: [KEY_INGREDIENT] के जरिए [MECHANISM], जिससे [SPECIFIC_RESULT]।

### SECTION 8 — Social Proof / Reviews Block
**Descriptive Name:** Review Wall — Mass Validation

**DR Purpose:**  
Final wall of trust before the end CTA. Uses multiple proof mechanisms simultaneously: aggregate rating, star distribution bar chart (shows transparency), individual named reviews with photos (humanises), and mix of languages (authentic, not corporate-polished).

**Copy Formula:**  
- **Aggregate Rating Display:** Big number + star rating graphic + total count  
- **Rating Distribution Chart:** 5★ to 1★ bar graph (shows overwhelmingly positive, but not fake-perfect)  
- **Individual Reviews:** Photo + Name + Stars + Short quote in natural language  
- **Authentic Mix:** Hindi, English, Hinglish — signals real users, not copywriters  
- **Volume Signal:** "999+ Reviews" heading implies so many they lost count

**Design Pattern:**  
- Rating summary at top: Large "999+" heading + star image + "X.X Rating Out of 5"  
- Star distribution: Horizontal progress bars, 5→1, with counts on right  
- Review cards: Profile photo (real, not stock-looking), name as heading, star rating, 1-3 line quote  
- Grid or vertical scroll of reviews  
- Final CTA button (⚡) at section close

**Template Copy:**
```
AGGREGATE BLOCK:

## DESIGN SYSTEM REFERENCE

### Colour Palette
| Role | Colour | Hex |
|------|--------|-----|
| Primary CTA / Accent | Amber Gold | `#F0A500` |
| Background (hero) | Near Black | `#0D0D0D` |
| Card/overlay backgrounds | Dark Charcoal | `rgba(20,20,20,0.85)` |
| Ticker bar | Warm Gold | `#D4900A` |
| Body text | White | `#FFFFFF` |
| Secondary text | Light grey | `#CCCCCC` |
| Star icons | Amber | `#F0A500` |

### Typography
| Element | Style |
|---------|-------|
| Hero Headline | Extra Bold, 36-48px, White |
| Sub-headlines | SemiBold, 20-24px, White |
| Section headings | Bold, 24-32px |
| Body copy | Regular, 16-18px, line-height 1.6 |
| CTA Button text | Bold, 18-20px, Black on Gold |

### CTA Button
- Shape: Rounded pill (`border-radius: 50px`)
- Colour: Amber Gold background, Black text
- Size: Large, full-width within container
- Icon: Phone icon (📞) for call CTAs
- Placement: Appears at EVERY section break — roughly every 200-300px of scroll

### Layout
- Max content width: ~600-700px, centred
- Mobile-first (single column)
- Hero: Full-viewport, background image with right-aligned overlay card
- Sections: Clean alternating dark backgrounds, generous padding (40-60px)
- Ticker: Full-bleed, amber background, no padding
- Sticky header: Fixed top, full-width

### Photography & Imagery
- Hero image: Lifestyle/aspirational — bedroom, intimate but tasteful, warm candlelight tones
- Reviewer photos: Real-looking, diverse Indian men, casual
- Avoid: Clinical/medical imagery, white lab coats (too pharmaceutical)

## SECTION ORDER SUMMARY

```
1. Sticky Header Bar          → Phone CTA always visible
2. Scrolling Ticker           → Desire loop, social proof in motion
3. Hero Section               → Big claim + rating + CTA (above fold)
4. Product Intro / Origin     → What is it, global trust, now local
5. Expert Video Carousel      → Third-party authority (video)
6. Why Choose Us              → 4-point objection handler
7. Benefit Stack              → 7+ outcome promises
8. Review Wall                → Mass social validation + final CTA
```
